1. Introduction

MoodiGo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

Data Type Purpose Legal Basis
Email Address Account creation, authentication, communication Contract performance
Name Personalization, account management Contract performance
Profile Information App personalization and user experience Legitimate interest

2.2 Location Information

With your explicit consent, we collect:

  • Precise Location: To provide nearby activity recommendations
  • Location History: To improve recommendation accuracy (stored locally)

You can disable location services at any time through your device settings.

2.3 Usage Information

  • App interactions and feature usage

With your permission, we access:

  • Photo Library: To select images for personalized content

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: Deliver personalized activity recommendations
  • Account Management: Create and maintain your user account
  • Personalization: Customize content based on your mood and preferences

  • Analytics: Improve app performance and user experience
  • Security: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in these circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • OpenAI: AI-powered recommendation engine
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Apple Maps: Location and mapping services

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access limitations to authorized personnel only
  • Regular Audits: Ongoing security assessments and improvements
  • Data Minimization: We collect only necessary information

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and as required by law:

  • Account Data: Until account deletion
  • Usage Analytics: Up to 2 years
  • Location Data: Processed in real-time, not permanently stored

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Account Control

  • Access: View your personal information
  • Update: Modify your profile and preferences
  • Delete: Request account and data deletion

7.2 Privacy Controls

  • Location Services: Enable/disable in device settings

  • Data Export: Request your data in portable format
